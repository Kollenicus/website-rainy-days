Our Story

Rainy Days was born in the heart of Bergen, a place known for
its rainy weather and ever-changing skies. Founded in 2024
by local outdoor enthusiasts and fashion lovers, we saw the
need for rain jackets that offer both style and protection.

Quality Materials

At Rainy Day we use weatherproof fabrics that
offer exceptional protection against the rain.
Our jackets are crafted from durable, breathable
materials like recycled polyester and waterproof
membranes, ensuring comfort, durability, and
sustainability. Each piece is designed to keep you
dry, no matter the conditions, while maintaining a
lightweight, stylish feel.

Our Promise To You

At Rainy Days, our goal is simple: to provide you
with rainwear that combines style, comfort, and
protection. We’re committed to keeping you dry
and confident, no matter the weather, while
delivering products that are built to last. Your
satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do.
